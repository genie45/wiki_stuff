{{FP heading|What is ARK?}}
As a man or woman stranded, naked, freezing, and starving on the unforgiving shores of a mysterious island called [[ARK: Survival Evolved|ARK]], use your skill and cunning to kill or tame and ride the plethora of leviathan dinosaurs and other primeval [[creatures]] roaming the land. Hunt, harvest resources, [[Crafting|craft]] items, [[Farming|grow crops]], [[Engrams|research technologies]], and [[Building|build shelters]] to withstand the [[Weather|elements]] and store valuables, all while [[Tribes|teaming up]] with (or preying upon) hundreds of other players to survive, dominate... and escape! '''[[ARK: Survival Ascended]]''' is the upcoming remastered version of [[ARK: Survival Evolved]], to be released in October 2023. '''[[ARK: Survival Evolved]]''' is the original game, released on August 29th, 2017 after being over two years in 'Early Access' and is available on [http://store.steampowered.com/app/346110 Steam], the [http://store.xbox.com/en-US/Xbox-One/Games/ARK-Survival-Evolved/70e3ab21-a3ba-4946-82e9-df7b5a72e363 Xbox Live Marketplace], PlayStation, mobile devices, the Nintendo Switch, and the [https://www.epicgames.com/store/en-US/product/ark/home Epic Games Store].

[[ARK 2|ARK II]], starring Vin Diesel as Santiago Da Costa, will be released for next-generation platforms in 2024. ARK II thrusts players into a brutal new world of primitive survival against both beast and man, facing down the ever-present threats of hyper-realistic intelligent dinosaurs, aggressive alien fauna, and the exotic environments of an unearthly planet. Survivors will continue [[ARK: Survival Evolved|ARK]]'s dramatic eons-spanning story of humankind's evolution, in this next-generation sandbox survival experience!

{{FP heading|ARK: The Animated Series}}
Releasing in 2023, [[ARK: The Animated Series]] chronicles the story of a mysterious primeval land populated by dinosaurs and other extinct creatures, where people from throughout human history have been resurrected. When 21st century Australian paleontologist [[Helena Walker]] awakes on the [[ARKs|ARK]] after tragedy, she must learn to survive and find new allies, or die again at the hands of ruthless warlords -- all while trying to uncover the true nature of their strange new world.
